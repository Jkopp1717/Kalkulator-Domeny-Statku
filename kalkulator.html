Metoda Wzór Wyniki
Ship’s Domain Depth - Metoda C.B. Barrasa na osiadanie statku, z ograniczeniem metody: $$0,5\leq C_{b} \leq 0,9; 0 \leq \frac{t}{L} \leq 0,005; 1,1 \leq \frac{h}{T} \leq 1,4;$$ $$SD_{D} = n\times T_{max}+\frac{m\times C_{B}}{30}\times (\frac{B\times T}{b\times h-B\times T})^{\frac{2}{3}}\times V^{2.08}+k\times h_{f}$$
